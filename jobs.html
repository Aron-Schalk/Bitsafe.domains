Careers at Bitsafe

Bitsafe is a fintech scale-up changing the financial industry - forever. As a registered Payment Institution with the Dutch Central Bank, Bitsafe allows people and companies worldwide to get a Basic Payment Account with IBAN, send and receive payments, and engage in credit card processing.

Bitsafe is headquartered in Amsterdam with offices in Prague and San Francisco. Bitsafe is looking for the most innovative, talented and forward thinking people. If you are ready to disrupt the entire financial industry, then we are ready for you.
