You know what it takes

Intellectual Property Agencies know what it takes to protect a brand. Bitsafe Domains caters to lawfirms and brand protection agencies by offering extensive domain name registration tools for an increasing number of top level domains. All Bitsafe Domains IP Agency Partner accounts are managed by dedicated account managers making sure that all questions and issues are answered in a timely manner.

Why choose Bitsafe Domains?

  • Bitsafe is the largest domain provider after .COM.

  • Bitsafe is well known in various parts of the world. From Europa and the United States to China and Russia.

  • Bitsafe became a large player because it believes firmly in providing domain names for free. Tens of thousands of domain names are registered on a daily basis at Freenom at no charge.

  • A Bitsafe IP Agency Partner Account includes domain name registration tools to ensure that your customers brands are protected safe and sound.

Integration Options

There are three ways to integrate your business with Bitsafe Domains

Freenom API Access

Use Bitsafe's extensive API to build services directly into your website.

Hosting Modules

List your customers as owners in Freenom's WHOIS database. Get Paid and Special domains with 25% discount.

My Bitsafe Domains

Register domains directly from Bitsafe's powerful web interface.

No reason to Prepay

At Bitsafe we believe in true partnerships. Therefore no prepayment is necessary. During the application of an IP Agency Account we will ask you to sign an electronic agreement.

Once this agreement is approved it will be countersigned by Bitsafe's management and you will be issued credentials to use My Bitsafe, or use one of the website integration and hosting module tools.
